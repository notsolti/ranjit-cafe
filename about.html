6+ Years of Love

How It All Started

From a Dream to Your Daily Cup

Ranjit Cafe started in 2018 as a small tea stall in Tilottama, Butwal. The founder, Ranjit Ji, had one simple dream - to create a space where people could sit, sip good tea or coffee, eat wholesome food, and just breathe.

Over the years, that humble corner has grown into a beloved local cafe, serving hundreds of guests every week. From students grabbing breakfast before class to families meeting over momos - Ranjit Cafe has become part of Tilottama's daily life.

Today, we remain proudly local - sourcing ingredients from nearby markets, hiring from the community, and making every dish by hand, just as it was on day one.
